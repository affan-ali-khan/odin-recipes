Milkshake

Chick-fil-A describes it as "warm fall flavors like cinnamon" blended with "crunchy bits of brown sugar spice cookies". Instead of leaning into the season's major produce players like pumpkin and apple, the restaurant took a more neutral flavor stance by playing up classic fall baking spices.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Cinnamon
  • Baking Spices

Steps

  1. Blend milk with ice.
  2. Add sugar and pumpkin spices.
  3. Add cinnamon and blend some more.
  4. Pour in a glass and enjoy.
