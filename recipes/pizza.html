Pizza

Big, meaty portobello mushrooms have substituted for burgers before, and now they want to own your pizza game. Who are we to say they can't play? And the reviews? They "love love love" it!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Start with a crust
  2. Add a sauce.
  3. Add some veggies.
  4. Add some protein.
  5. Add cheese.
  6. Bake your creation in a hot oven (450 F or above).
